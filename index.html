About

Dyenum's Store was opened in January, 2015 with the aim of selling all things clothing to customers. By 2020 we had already grown to more than 5 locations around the country; Lagos, Abuja, Port Harcourt, Kaduna and Enugu. Due to the Pandemic in early 2020 which forced businesses to close around the world, we launched our online store in March 2020 to enable our customers order items right on our online store and have them delivered to any location within the country within three business days. This enabled us to still continue to serve our loyal customers while our physical locations remained closed. Over the last 5 years we have experienced lots of progress, opening more physical branches in three more states and also improving our online presence through various campaigns on the internet.
